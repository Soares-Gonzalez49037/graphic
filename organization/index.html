What does the ASG Graphics Organization do?

The ASG Graphics Organization helps graphic designers find jobs, to work for various clients. We help the graphic designers with their work because members will have access to the latest technologies.



Who are the clients?

The clients range from anyone who needs help with advertisement and promotional. Sometimes the client needs help with animation, logos, or even billboards.




How do members get paid?

Members are paid base on their work for the client. This means the client pays the member not the organization. This organization can help the member advocate a price to their client though.
